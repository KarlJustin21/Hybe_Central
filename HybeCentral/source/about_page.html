ILLIT (아일릿; also stylized as :ll:+) is a five-member
multinational girl group under Belift Lab.
They were formed through the JTBC reality show R U Next?
Initially consisting of six members,
they debuted on March 25, 2024, with the mini album
"Super Real Me."

The group's name, ILLIT, is derived from the phrase "I Will Be It."
It combines a sense of autonomous and proactive intention
("I will") with the pronoun "It," representing something special.
Depending on the verb placed between these words,
the phrase can take on various meanings.

OUR STORY

Background and History

HYBE Central was born out of a school project, envisioned by a group of passionate fans who wanted to combine their love for Hallyu
culture with their learning journey in web development. As students delving into HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, and MySQL, we sought to
create a platform that not only showcased our technical skills but also served a meaningful purpose for the global fan community.

Inspiration and motivation behind HYBE Central

Our inspiration stemmed from the desire to build a comprehensive and engaging fan site that could cater to both new and long-time
fans of HYBE Entertainment. We noticed a gap in the online fan experience and wanted to create a space where fans could easily access
the latest news, exclusive content, and connect with others who share their passion.

The motivation behind HYBE Central was twofold: to develop a high-quality website as a testament to our growing skills in web
development and to provide a valuable resource for the vibrant Hallyu fandom. Through this project, we aim to foster a deeper
connection between fans and their favorite artists while also enhancing our own learning and expertise in creating dynamic, user-
friendly web applications.

COMMUNITY GUIDELINES

Code of Conduct for User

At HYBE Central, we strive to create a welcoming, safe, and enjoyable
environment for all fans. To maintain this, we ask all users to adhere to the
following code of conduct:

  1. Respect for Others: Treat all members with kindness and respect.
    Abusive, harassing, or discriminatory behavior will not be tolerated.

  2. No Hate Speech: Any content that promotes hate speech, violence, or
    discrimination against individuals or groups based on race, ethnicity,
    religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or any other characteristic
    will be removed.

  3. No Spamming: Avoid posting repetitive content or spamming comments
    and discussions. Keep the community clean and relevant to everyone’s
    interests.

  4. Authenticity: Post original content and give proper credit where it’s due.
    Do not share false information, rumors, or unverified news.

  5. Privacy: Respect the privacy of others. Do not share personal
    information about other users without their explicit consent.

  6. No inappropriate Content: Avoid posting content that is sexually
    explicit, violent, or otherwise inappropriate for a broad audience.

Violations of these guidelines may result in warnings, temporary
suspension, or permanent banning from the platform, depending on
the severity of the offense.

Guidelines for Respectful Engagement

To foster a positive and engaging community, we encourage all users to follow
these guidelines for respectful engagement:

  1. Constructive Discussions: When engaging in discussions, aim to be
    constructive and positive. Offer thoughtful comments and avoid negative or
    hostile interactions.

  2. Support and Encouragement: Support fellow fans by liking and positively
    commenting on their posts. Encourage others to share their thoughts and
    experiences.

  3. Disagree Respectfully: It's okay to disagree, but do so respectfully. Avoid
    personal attacks and focus on discussing ideas rather than criticizing
    individuals.

  4. Stay On-Topic: Keep discussions relevant to the topic at hand. Off-topic
    comments can disrupt the flow of conversation and detract from the
    community experience.

  5. Use Appropriate Language: Refrain from using profanity or offensive
    language. Maintain a respectful tone at all times.

  6. Report Violations: If you come across any content or behavior that violates
    these guidelines, report it to our moderators. Your cooperation helps us keep
    the community safe and enjoyable for everyone.

By following these guidelines, you help ensure that HYBE Central remains a
friendly, engaging, and inclusive space for all fans to connect and celebrate
their shared passions.
