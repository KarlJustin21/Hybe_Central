SEVENTEEN TO BECOME UNESCO’s FIRST-EVER GOODWILL AMBASSADOR FOR YOUTH

Source : HYBE CORPORATION , hybe official website

On June 26, SEVENTEEN will be appointed UNESCO’s Goodwill Ambassador for Youth by
Director-General Audrey Azoulay at UNESCO Headquarters in Paris. This follows their
significant participation in the 13th UNESCO Youth Forum in November 2023, where
they emphasized youth solidarity through a speech and performance. SEVENTEEN's
commitment to youth empowerment aligns with UNESCO's mission, demonstrated by their
collaborative 'GoingTogether' initiative in 2022, which supported educational projects
in Timor-Leste and Malawi. As Goodwill Ambassadors, SEVENTEEN will use their influence
to spotlight youth challenges and promote change through UNESCO programs, unveiling a
new partnership to support global youth projects.
